The Mission of the PTO

PTO's mission is to promote open and positive communication and understanding between parents and staff of the Silsbee Middle School. Our efforts serve to enhance and maximize the education of every child while aiding them in achieving their highest potential.

Nestled in the south east corner of Texas you'll find the charming small town of Silsbee. With a population just shy of 7,000 you'll find our Edwards Johnson Memorial Silsbee Middle School. Our middle school has 6th grade, 7th grade and 8th grade students. It was built in 1967. Our small town was first referred to as "Mill Town" but later was named Silsbee after Nathaniel Devereux Silsbee. Its address is 415 HWY 327 West Silsbee, TX 77656 Its phone number is 409-980-7897
