President

Miss Monique is serving as our PTO president. She is a Silsbee native. She currently has 3 kids attending Silsbee Middle School. In her free time she loves to paint and bake cookies. Her duties as president are as follows: presides over meetings of the board and General PTO, coordinates work of all PTO members to ensure duties are being carried out, sign contracts and checks, serves as liaison between PTO and Administration/Staff.

Vice President

Miss Frankie is serving as our PTO Vice President. She has lived in Silsbee for 12 years. She currently has one child attending Silsbee Middle School. In her free time she loves to go swimming and paint. Her duties as vice president are as follows: assists the president in her duties, perform duties of the president in the case the president is unable to attend.Participate in board meetings and provide input for decisions.Be an ambassador for the PTO and the school.Assist in monitoring PTO Google Email Account with President and Corresponding Secretary.

Treasurer

Miss Katrina is serving as our PTO Treasurer. She is new to Silsbee as of 3 years ago. She currently has 2 kids attending Silsbee Middle School. In her free time she like to go camping and loves playing board games. Her duties as treasurer are as follows: The PTO treasurer is the custodian of the parent group's funds: helps develop the budget, collects fundraising money, writes checks for purchases and reimbursements, tracks income and spending, makes financial reports, and organizes the books for annual audit.
